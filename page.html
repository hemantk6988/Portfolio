Idly is a popular South Indian breakfast dish made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. Steamed into soft, fluffy cakes, idly is typically served with chutney and sambar. It's nutritious, light, and easily digestible, making it a staple in many households and a favorite across India.

Dosa is a beloved South Indian crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. Crisp and golden, dosas are typically served with coconut chutney and sambar. Versatile and delicious, they can be plain or filled with spiced potatoes, making them a popular breakfast or snack choice.
